About LGPD

Dedicated to empowering persons with disabilities through advocacy, support, and community building since 2020.

Our Foundation

The Life Gate for Persons with Disabilities (LGPD) was established on December 15, 2020, by 11 dedicated members from different districts, known as the founders of our organization. We began with a shared vision of creating meaningful change in our community.

Our Location

Our organization operates in Kibenga cell, Ndera Sector in Gasabo district, serving as a beacon of hope and support for persons with disabilities throughout the region.

Our Vision

"To see the persons with disabilities empowered for life transformation"

Our Mission

To advocate, empower and transform the broken lives of people with disabilities through sustainable development programs and well-being in partnership with local community.

What We Do

Our comprehensive approach focuses on empowerment, advocacy, and transformation through various programs and services.

Love & Care

We provide compassionate care and emotional support to individuals and families, creating a nurturing environment where everyone feels valued and understood.

Training & Education

Comprehensive training programs including life skills, trauma healing, sexual reproductive health education, and vocational training for sustainable empowerment.

Advocacy & Awareness

We advocate for the rights of persons with disabilities and raise awareness in communities to promote inclusion and fight discrimination.

Life Transformation

Through our holistic approach, we empower individuals to overcome challenges and transform their lives, building stronger, more inclusive communities.

Our Impact

Since our establishment in 2020, we have been committed to creating lasting change in the lives of persons with disabilities. Through our dedicated programs, we continue to build bridges of understanding, provide essential support, and advocate for equal rights and opportunities for all.

Our Objectives

Six strategic objectives that drive our mission to create lasting positive change in the lives of persons with disabilities.

1

Life Transformation Training

Empowering persons with disabilities through comprehensive training programs including healing trauma wounds, life skills development, sexual reproductive health education, and personal empowerment sessions.

2

Community Strengthening

Contributing to the reconstitution of groups of disabled people to strengthen their capacities, advancing their rights and combating various forms of violence and discrimination against them.

3

Advocacy & Support

Raising awareness and assisting vulnerable families, orphans, street children, with special focus on advocating for women and girls with disabilities and children from related families.

4

Job Creation Initiatives

Supporting persons with disabilities' initiatives for job creation through various professional formations, apprenticeship training programs, and entrepreneurship development.

5

Strategic Partnerships

Partnering and networking with other organizations, government agencies, and international bodies aimed at improving the well-being of persons with disabilities and elderly vulnerable populations.

6

Sustainable Development

Sustaining development programs and well-being of persons with disabilities through education, healthcare, agro-livestock projects, and animal husbandry initiatives for long-term empowerment.

Join Our Mission

These objectives represent our commitment to creating a more inclusive society. Together, we can achieve lasting change and ensure that every person with disabilities has the opportunity to thrive and contribute meaningfully to their communities.
